Leather Wax Recipe

Heat the leather pieces you wish to wax in a warm oven (around 180F)

Take equal parts by weight beeswax and raw linseed oil. Heat them together in a double-boiler, mixing occasionally until fully melted. Add about 1/10 part by volume of turpentine to the hot mixture.

Take the hot leather out of the oven one piece at a time and brush the hot mixture into it until no more will absorb. Replace the piece in the oven and continue on to the next piece. Repeat until no more
