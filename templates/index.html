Inspired and tested by the trail

I've been making handmade leather shoes since 2008: In the spring of 2020, I made these hiking boots. Since then, those boots and I have hiked 2,500 miles together:

  • Santiam, Ore. to South Lake Tahoe, Cali. on the Pacific Crest Trail,
  • Denver to Leadville on the Colorado Trail, and
  • Springer Mtn., Ga. to Bennington, VT on the Appalachan Trail.
